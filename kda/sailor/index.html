#AboutUs

Sailor is a national trip website that allows Congolese people of DRC to book a place for transport facilities including Airplanes, Ships, and ,Bus. In fact, this website is one of multiple solution provide by the GuideonTech company, an high technology company that offer multiple tech solution to congolese including mainly websites, mobile apps, graphic designs, and many other.

We have 3 principale booking for transport facilities; Airplanes, Ships and But. Each transport facilities have one or more company to manage it. We are the portal and the facilitator between you and the companies that will help you to book in any company of any transport facility you want.
